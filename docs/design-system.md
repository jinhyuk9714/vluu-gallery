# Design System

## Tone

- Editorial minimal, with large image surfaces and restrained typography
- Work-first hierarchy: images lead, copy supports
- Calm transitions only; no parallax-heavy storytelling

## Typography

- Heading: `Libre Bodoni`
- Body and UI: `Public Sans`
- Body copy should stay compact and readable, never dominate the image field

## Palette

- Background: warm off-white
- Foreground: charcoal
- Accent: muted olive and steel
- Cards and chrome should be nearly invisible

## Layout Rules

- Prefer asymmetrical grids over auto-masonry
- Keep generous vertical rhythm and whitespace around section boundaries
- Mobile defaults to one-column reading and viewing flow

## Motion

- Hover and entrance transitions: `150ms` to `250ms`
- Animate opacity and translate only
- Remove non-essential motion for `prefers-reduced-motion`

## Avoid

- Decorative frames around every image
- Glossy shadows and dashboard-like cards
- Dense metadata blocks or long text paragraphs on image-first screens
